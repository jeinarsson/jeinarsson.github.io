About me

Engineer, hacker, physicist, dad

I’m currently finishing a Ph.D in theoretical physics at the University of Gothenburg, Sweden. It’s about the mechanics of small particles suspended in fluids. Mathematical Physics is great fun and I should write about it sometime.

I like to hack on stuff, both soft- and hardware. When I do I’m always amazed at the amount of documentation available on the web: Blog posts, forums, stack exchanges and so on. An aim of this blog is to give some of that back whenever I find an opportunity.

I grew up in countryside Sweden. You know, red house, deep forest, growing tasty food, and nowadays also gigabit fiber internet. I like it a lot and after finishing my Ph.D I’ll work on making a living out here for me and my family.
